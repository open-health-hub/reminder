    Got any questions?

    Have a look through our Frequently Asked Questions below, or contact us if you need more information.

    What is Take Your Meds?

    Take Your Meds is a telephone reminder service to help you remember to take your medication. You can arrange receive reminder calls or text messages to any UK phone number, whether a landline or a mobile.

    Who is Take Your Meds for?

    Take Your Meds is for anyone who would like help remembering to take their medication, or for the family or friends of someone who takes medication.

    We particularly want to help people who don't use a smartphone.

    How does it work?

    You can arrange reminders for yourself, or on behalf of someone you care about, or even both.

    First, register for an account with Take Your Meds, then tell us what phone number to contact, when you would like to receive your reminders, and whether you'd prefer a call or a text message. You can record a new voice message to send, or use one of our built-in messages. That's it!

    How much does it cost?

    For now, Take Your Meds is totally free to use, so why not get started with your reminders today!

    We hope never to have to charge end-users for the service, and aim to cover our costs another way.

    I'm worried about spam. Will you use my phone number or my details in any other way?

    Short answer: no. See our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy for the longer story.

    We will never contact you unless you ask us to. If you sign up with your email address we will assume you are happy to receive occasional updates from us, but you can unsubscribe from these if you want to.
