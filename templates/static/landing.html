What We Offer

Customisable schedule

Tell us when you need your reminders and let us do the work for you. Whether you need 1 or 100 reminders a day, we can help.

Record your own message

Add your own reminder message, or you could ask a family member to record one for you. You can also write a custom text message if that's what you'd prefer.

Keep track of your reminders and if they are acknowledged, so you can easily see how medication is being taken.

Why use Take Your Meds?

Taking medication is hard

Even remembering to take one pill in the morning can be difficult, but add in another pill three times a day and another one at bedtime and things soon get very complicated.

Not all medications are tablets

If you need to use a particular mouthwash, apply a cream, replace a patch or use anything that isn't a tablet then classic pill-helper boxes can't help.

Not everyone uses a smartphone

All you need in order to use Take Your Meds is a phone number, whether a landline or a mobile. You can receive phonecalls or text messages as you prefer.
